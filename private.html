Itinerary Themes

Where shall we begin?

The Golden Triangle

Delhi, Agra and Jaipur for first-time travellers. Six to ten days of monuments, marigolds and an unforgettable dawn at the Taj.

Royal Rajasthan

Two to three weeks through Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, Jaisalmer — heritage havelis, lake palaces, camel dunes at Khuri.

Kerala & the South

Backwater houseboats, Cochin spice markets, hill-station tea plantations, Ayurvedic retreats on the Malabar coast.

The Himalayan North

Ladakh's monasteries, Shimla's colonial bones, the toy train to Darjeeling, Bhutan as an extension if time allows.

Wildlife & Wilderness

Tigers at Ranthambore and Bandhavgarh, leopards at Jhalana, the one-horned rhinoceros at Kaziranga, jungle lodges throughout.

Festivals & Fairs

Holi in Mathura, Diwali in Jaipur, the Pushkar camel fair, Onam in Kerala, the Hornbill Festival in Nagaland — timed and built around the date.
