Boutique stays, hand-chosen.

A Country, A Library

Twenty-eight states.
Seven thousand temples.
One lifetime.

India is not a destination — it is a hundred destinations layered over each other, separated by language, climate, faith and cuisine.

We help you choose where to begin. The Golden Triangle for first-time travellers. Rajasthan for romance and royal history. Kerala's backwaters for slowness. The Himalayas for silence. Tamil Nadu for temples that have never stopped breathing.

Tell us what moves you — we'll build the rest.
